Approach

Partner Factories

We will link you directly with our strategic manufacturing partners in Afrika, India and Asia. All our partners are fully integrated and certified producers.

Global awards are received for Fair Wage, Zero Discharge Hazardous Chemicals, Eco Wash, Water Leadership Plan and Eco Dying.

Together with our partners we are are constantly developing new yarns. We are aware of ideal price points and endeavor to meet without compromising on content. We do not promise, but work to achieve.

Being fully integrated means we can control everything from yarn, knitting, weaving, dying to actual garment manufacturing, all the way back to you, closing the loop.

Community Support

The partnership with our factories includes that 1% of sales goes back to the garment workers communities. Supporting local communities with things like education and healthcare.
